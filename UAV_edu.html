Drones for Education

Empowering learners through UAV training, workshops, and community engagement.

Open Access Drone Course

Open-access drone mapping training course to teach research partners and broader communities how to collect remote sensing data. This training covers drone operations, mapping principles, safety guidelines, preflight planning, FAA Part 107 exam prep, image stitching, and GIS-based analysis.

Drone with MSTI K12 Summer Camp

Drone and GIS technologies are utilized for K12 education in the Mineta Summer Transportation Institute. Students earn college credits and learn drone operations and aerial mapping under Dr. Bo Yang's instruction.

SAVi Drone Workshops

SAVI's drone workshop covers drone operations, flight planning, airspace regulations, imagery processing, and data management, with follow-up self-study for FAA Part 107 exam preparation. (Free for SJSU students)
