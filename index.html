Open Access Drone Training Course

GeoFly Lab proudly offers a comprehensive, free drone training course on GitHub. This course covers autonomous mapping with Drone2Map/Pix4D, DJI Terra and GS Pro, ArcGIS series and Google Earth Engine, and includes an FAA Part 107 study guide. It’s designed to equip you with the skills needed for drone mapping and to empower you as a citizen scientist.
